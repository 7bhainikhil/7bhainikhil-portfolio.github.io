SEMANTIC SEGMENTATION OF SATELLITE IMAGE

The developed image segmentation model, rooted in the U-Net family of deep neural networks (CNNs), is versatile in addressing various applications. One primary use case involves multi-class labeling challenges, specifically in identifying and labeling land use and land cover in satellite images. Moreover, the model excels in single-class segmentation tasks, demonstrating its efficacy in distinguishing and delineating specific features such as floodwater or fire against complex backgrounds. This adaptability positions the U-Net-based model as a powerful tool for both comprehensive land cover analysis. and focused identification of specific environmental phenomena.
